The {{mitochondria}} is the powerhouse of the cell.

{{DNA}} is stored in the {{nucleus}} of eukaryotic cells.

Cells obtain energy through {{cellular respiration}}, which occurs in three stages:
{{1.1::glycolysis}}, {{1.2::the Krebs cycle}}, and {{1.3::oxidative phosphorylation}}.
